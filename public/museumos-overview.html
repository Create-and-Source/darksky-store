What is MuseumOS?

MuseumOS replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, email threads, paper forms, and disconnected software that most cultural institutions use to operate. It's one system that runs your entire organization.

What it replaces
  • Spreadsheets for tracking inventory, orders, and members
  • Email chains between departments for internal communication
  • Paper forms for field trip bookings and volunteer sign-ups
  • Separate software for email marketing, social media, and design
  • Manual QuickBooks data entry from receipts and invoices
  • Printed schedules and whiteboards for volunteer coordination
  • Multiple logins across Square, Mailchimp, Canva, and Google Sheets

The Big Idea

Every person in your organization — from the Executive Director to a weekend volunteer — logs into one platform and sees exactly what they need. Nothing more, nothing less.

🔧 What's Left to Connect

The entire front-end is built and functional. These backend connections are the only remaining work.

💳 Square Payments
Connect the POS and online checkout to Square for real credit card processing. The UI is built — just needs the API key.
📚 QuickBooks API
Replace CSV exports with direct sync. Nancy clicks "Sync" and data flows into QuickBooks automatically.
📧 Email Delivery
Connect email composer to SendGrid or Mailchimp. Emails are composed in the platform — just need delivery.
📱 SMS Delivery
Connect text blasts to Twilio. UI is built with templates and audience targeting — just needs the API.
🔒 Real Authentication
Replace demo login with real user accounts. Passwords, sessions, and security.
🗃 Database
Move from browser localStorage to a real database (PostgreSQL or Supabase). Data persists across devices and browsers.

What This Means

The platform you see is not a mockup or a prototype. It is a working application with real data flow, real business logic, and real user experiences. The backend connections listed above are standard integrations that take weeks, not months.
